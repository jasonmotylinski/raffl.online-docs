    Creating Your Raffl.online Account

    Follow these steps to create a new Raffl.online account.

    Step 1: Go to the Sign Up Page

    Navigate to raffl.online/signup to access the registration form.

    Step 2: Enter Your Information

    Fill out the registration form with the following:

    • Email Address – This will be your username for logging in.
    • Password – Must meet the requirements below.
    • Confirm Password – Re-enter your password to confirm.

    Password Requirements

    Your password must:

    • Be at least 8 characters long
    • Include at least one uppercase letter
    • Include at least one lowercase letter
    • Include at least one number
    • Include at least one special character (e.g., !@#$%^&*)

    Step 3: Verify Your Email

    After submitting the form:

    1. Check your email inbox for a verification message from Raffl.online.
    2. Click the verification link in the email.
    3. The link expires after 10 minutes, so verify promptly.

    If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

    Step 4: Verification Complete

    Once you click the verification link, your account is activated. You can now log in at raffl.online/admin using your email and password.

    Resending Verification Email

    If your verification link expired or you didn't receive the email:

    1. Go to the sign up page.
    2. Click the link to resend the verification email.
    3. Enter your email address and submit.
    4. A new verification email will be sent.
